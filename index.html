Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is basically an ERP software from Microsoft Inc. which includes a rich array of features to enable businesses across the world spanning industries to emerge more sophisticated and more professional by tapping upon their underlying product or service expertise potential. Dynamics 365, as the term 365 suggests provides best and comprehensive view of all of the business modules, be it marketing, customer service, finance and operations, field service, inventory control and so forth, but in precise detail. Microsoft Dynamics 365 solves many of the problems companies face daily. It eliminates the need for problem-solving processes that take a long time.

The software can connect to email, calendar, and contact data because Outlook / Exchange can be synchronized.

The platform also has social listening tools that you can use to find out what your contacts are talking about.
