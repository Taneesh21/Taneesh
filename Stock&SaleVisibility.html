Stock & Sales Visibility in the Channel

Agenda

Every principle company wanted to track the sales & stock with their distributors with following objects:

  1. Based on the correct stock in the market, the principle company can do the efficient production planning.
  2. Principle company can track in which town, which distributors are effective and which products are getting sold.
  3. Principle company can make the schemes or can do the marketing activities in week areas.
  4. Principle company plans their raw materials based on the actual sales date, not from the date they sold to the dealer. If the dealer sells the materials in one month from the date of billing, then the company can plan to do the production accordingly, which will decrease the working capital, meaning the company will have direct ROI coming.
  5. In other words, SSV is the backbone of the company to plan production & import, what is getting sold in the market based on actual sales by the partners/Channel.
  6. Currently, companies are making yearly sales plans based on last year, quarter basis, which is not a foolproof way to making the plan.

Problems in Capturing the Data

  1. Its required heavy investment in Infrastructure with recurring expenses in IT.
  2. How we will enable/train to channel partners, especially in rural area’s.
  3. Channel partner in an area where internet connectivity is very poor.
  4. Channel partner is not ready to put the entry on our portal, because he is distributors for multiple products, if he keep doing the entry for all the companies then his most the times goes in this and he will not be able to the sales.
  5. It is difficult for companies to enable partners, as they are in to multi states, where different sales tax rules and languages applies
  6. Currently companies are making yearly sales plans based on last year, quarter basic, which is not full proof way to making the plan.
